Jules Lucero is a printmaker based out of Missoula, Montana specializing in relief prints. For Lucero, printmaking comes with a rich history taking inspiration from the styles of older print masters like Albrecht Durer and bringing them into a modern context. Lucero’s work presents not just an image, but a story, giving animals human traits and emotions to explore humanity’s relation with the environment. They serve as a reflection of everyday, precious moments; biking, pouring coffee or having a conversation in the rain.
